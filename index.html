Selected Publications

From Scenes to Semantics: PersianCLEVR for Bilingual 3D Visual Reasoning

1st Workshop on VLM4RWD@ NeurIPS 2025
Co-First Author
Kianoosh Vadaei, Melika Shirian, Arshia Hemmat, Mohammad Hassan Heydari, Ali Mamanpoosh, Afsaneh Fatemi

We introduce PersianClevr, the first bilingual benchmark for 3D visual reasoning in English and Persian, built from CLEVR, Super-CLEVR, and ClevrTex with new QA synthesis. It evaluates VLMs on attributes, counting, comparison, spatial relations, and logic, revealing strong surface understanding but clear weaknesses in true 3D and compositional reasoning.

3D VQA Multimodal Benchmark Dataset: PersianClevr
OpenReview

Adaptive Chunking for VideoRAG Pipelines with a Newly Gathered Bilingual Educational Dataset

CSICC 2025 (29th International Computer Conference, Computer Society of Iran), Tehran
Co-First Author
Arshia Hemmat, Kianoosh Vadaei, Melika Shirian, Mohammad Hassan Heydari, Afsaneh Fatemi

This paper presents an adaptive chunking strategy for VideoRAG pipelines using a newly collected bilingual educational dataset. We demonstrate improved performance in video question answering through optimized temporal segmentation techniques.

VideoQA RAG Multimodal Dataset: EduViQA
IEEE Xplore

Leveraging Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Persian University Knowledge Retrieval

2024 15th International Conference on Information and Knowledge Technology (IKT)
Co-First Author
Arshia Hemmat, Kianoosh Vadaei, Mohammad Hassan Heydari, Afsaneh Fatemi

We introduce a novel approach to Persian knowledge retrieval using RAG techniques, specifically designed for university-level educational content. Our system demonstrates significant improvements in accuracy and relevance for Persian language queries.

RAG Persian LLMs Dataset: UQB
