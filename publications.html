Adaptive Chunking for VideoRAG Pipelines with a Newly Gathered Bilingual Educational Dataset

CSICC 2025 (29th International Computer Conference, Computer Society of Iran), Tehran
Arshia Hemmat, Kianoosh Vadaei, Melika Shirian, Mohammad Hassan Heydari, Afsaneh Fatemi

This paper presents an adaptive chunking strategy for VideoRAG pipelines using a newly collected bilingual educational dataset. We demonstrate improved performance in video question answering through optimized temporal segmentation techniques. Our approach addresses the challenge of effectively processing long-form educational videos by implementing dynamic chunking based on content complexity and semantic boundaries.

Leveraging Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Persian University Knowledge Retrieval

arXiv:2411.06237v2 (2024)
Arshia Hemmat, Kianoosh Vadaei, Mohammad Hassan Heydari, Afsaneh Fatemi

We introduce a novel approach to Persian knowledge retrieval using RAG techniques, specifically designed for university-level educational content. Our system demonstrates significant improvements in accuracy and relevance for Persian language queries. The work addresses the unique challenges of Persian text processing and provides a comprehensive framework for educational knowledge retrieval in low-resource language settings.
